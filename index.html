Biography

I am a third-year undergraduate student majoring in Mathematics and Computer Science. I am currently attending Arcadia University (Philadelphia, PA) as a visiting exchange student, while enrolled at Jiangsu University[cite: 4, 6, 16].

My research focuses on AI Security and Trustworthy Agents. I am dedicated to developing principled defenses against attacks on Large Language Models (LLMs), such as prompt injection, and enhancing the robustness of agentic workflows. Leveraging my background in mathematics, I also work on building secure foundation models for high-stakes applications, including biomedicine and autonomous systems[cite: 50, 51, 52].

I am currently a Remote Research Intern at UCSD working on spatial reasoning in AI[cite: 10, 17].
