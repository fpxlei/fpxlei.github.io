Research Interests

AI Security Trustworthy Agents LLMs Medical Imaging

I am currently a senior undergraduate student majoring in Mathematics and Computer Science. I am pursuing a joint degree at Jiangsu University and Arcadia University (Visiting Exchange).

My primary research interests lie in AI Security and Trustworthy Agents. I am dedicated to developing principled defenses against attacks on Large Language Models (LLMs) and enhancing their robustness in agentic workflows. Additionally, leveraging my background in mathematics, I aim to build secure foundation models for high-stakes applications, including biomedicine and autonomous systems.

I am currently working as a Remote Research Intern at UCSD and NTU.
